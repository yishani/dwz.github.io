About

I'm a research scientist at FAIR, Meta, working on structured prediction, generative models, and reinforcement learning for LLMs. Previously, I was at Qualcomm AI Research in Amsterdam. I completed my PhD in Machine Learning at the University of Amsterdam as part of the ELLIS program, supervised by Cees Snoek and Gertjan Burghouts. Before that, I studied Computer Science at Technical University of Munich (MSc & BSc). I also spent time at KPMG as a consultant, including a stint in Panama.
