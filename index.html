About

I'm a research scientist at FAIR, Meta. Previously, I was at Qualcomm AI Research in Amsterdam. I completed my PhD at the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Cees Snoek and Gertjan Burghouts, as part of the ELLIS PhD program with TNO. My research focuses on structured prediction, generative models, and reinforcement learning for sequence models.
