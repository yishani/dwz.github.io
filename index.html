About

I'm a research scientist at FAIR, Meta in Paris, working on code generation and RL. I'm a core contributor to CWM. Previously, I was at Qualcomm AI Research in Amsterdam. I did my PhD in Machine Learning at the University of Amsterdam as part of the ELLIS program, supervised by Cees Snoek and Gertjan Burghouts.
